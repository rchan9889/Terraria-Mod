This mod adds a new damage class. Every tick, damage bonuses from this class is multiplied by a random value between 2 and -1.
Mod includes:
    2 new stats:
        Gambit: increases the maximum multiplier by 2 while decreasing the minimum by 1.
        Foresight: increases the minimum multiplier by 1.
    10 weapons
    4 armor sets
    7 accessories
